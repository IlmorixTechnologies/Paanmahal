About Paan Mahal

Founded in 2010, Paan Mahal has been serving authentic Indian paan to connoisseurs in Kurnool and beyond. Our journey began in the heart of Venkatramana Colony, where we mastered the art of perfect paan-making.

We source our paan leaves directly from the finest gardens in West Bengal and Kerala, ensuring you get the freshest and most flavorful leaves every time.
